Intelligent Joy慧悅財經

慧悅財經不僅擁有互聯網線上港股教學平台，更提供有線下財經課程。匯聚多位擁有多年經驗、在市場上馳名的金融資深專家作課程導師，打造多門優質的獨家財經課程。不僅教授CFA等財經專業課程，更設計如何分析財務報表、解讀經濟指標、匯金分析等實用性課程。

Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA)

The programme aims to teach students fundamental concepts and techniques of management accounting while also highlighting the diversity of approaches and practices of management accounting.

如何分析財務報表?

This programme provides insights in advanced management account, with reference to the advanced aspects of planning, decision-making, performance evaluation and control.

Students need to consider a range of strategic management accounting techniques and critically examine the impact of behavioural and social issues in the use and implementation of management accounting techniques.

如何解讀經濟指標?

The programme aims to enable students to prepare and examine financial reports in order to evaluate performance of the organization.

匯金基本分析精讀工作坊

The programme provides an opportunity for students to gain real world accounting expertise within an international context. It brings into focus the context of accounting information taking into consideration wider social, political, economic, natural and cultural environments.

Students can acquire an international perspective on contemporary issues relating to accounting and finance.

盧駿匡炒股實戰課程

The programme is a capstone project involving the development of business research skills and their application to real world business and accounting related problems.
